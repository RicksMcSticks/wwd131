This is all the fun things I do!

Surfing!

"Surfing is totally new for me, but that’s what makes it awesome. Me and my friends are all beginners, so every wipeout turns into a cheer session. It’s not about who’s the best out there—it’s about showing up, paddling out, and laughing when we nosedive together. Every small win, like actually standing up for a second, feels huge because we’ve got each other’s backs. The stoke is real, and even the falls are more fun when you’re surrounded by people who believe in you." 🏄‍♂️🌊🤙

Triathlon!

"My last triathlon was one for the books—not just because of the swim, bike, and run, but because I finally beat my dad. We’ve always had a bit of a friendly rivalry, and this time, all the training paid off. From the early morning start to the final sprint, I was locked in. Crossing that finish line ahead of him felt like a huge win—not just over him, but over every doubt and setback I’d pushed through. He gave me a proud smile and a high five, and I knew this race would be one I’d never forget." 🏊‍♂️🚴‍♂️🏃‍♂️🔥

Color Run!

"The Color Run is one of the most exciting 5Ks out there—less about speed and more about pure joy! You race through a vibrant course where people throw colored chalk at you at every kilometer, turning you into a running rainbow by the finish line. It’s all about having fun, making memories, and not taking yourself too seriously. Whether you’re sprinting or dancing your way through, it’s impossible not to smile when you’re getting blasted with color and high fives!" 🎨🏃‍♂️✨
